Here are samples of the updates your client can submit.

  • Expecting a Child

    Know when clients are expanding the family, to help them plan for their exciting future.

  • College Savings

    Help your clients plan to one of life’s major events: college. When your clients plan to begin saving, you will be notified, and can help them create a plan for success.

  • Vehicle

    Help your clients plan for vacation or an extended time away by recommending actions to assist them.

  • Travel Plans

    Help your clients plan for any vacation or extended time away. Advise them on expenses, and make a couple recommendations to assist them!

  • Marriage

    An engagement leading to a wedding is one of the largest events in a person’s life, however, most do not plan for unforeseen related expenses that may impact their financial standing. Help clients understand how to plan for events leading up to and following a wedding.

  • Separation

    Separation and divorce are times when financial turmoil is imminent. Help your clients plan through an event that affects all aspects of their lives.

  • Real Estate

    Real Estate tends to be one of the highest priced assets most people encounter. Help your clients navigate the complicated waters of the real-estate industry.

  • Renovations

    Renovations can be planned for until unforeseen events cause costs to skyrocket. Help your clients create a financial safety net to help them realize their vision.

  • Retirement

    Retirement plans can change for all kinds of reasons, from an expansion of the family, to a change in a person’s career. Reach out to clients to help them find the most optimal approach for their retirements.

  • Employment

    For most people, jobs and careers change over time. Help your client understand changes in cash flow and benefit palns to ensure they are balancing their immediate needs and long-term goals.

  • Health

    Unexpected health issues can arise at any time in life. Assist clients in planning for such periods by ensuring that they have all the resources they need to make it through intact financially.

  • Inheritance

    Inheritances and gifts can come all in all forms of assets. Help clients understand how to grow these assets and plan for the tax implications to keep them protected.

  • All Other Life Events

    For all other events, we give clients the ability to report anything else that may be occurring.
