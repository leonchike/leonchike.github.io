Exporting
Your Clients

Whether during your free trial or when you subscribe, you can easily export your clients from your CRM tool into KIVVIK.

SELECT YOUR CRM

IMPORT CLIENTS FROM WEALTHBOX

Boost the capabilities of Wealthbox’s dynamic system by pairing it with Kivvik’s robust “Know Your Client” system.

Step 1

Open your contacts

Select the Contacts from the Side Menu Navigation.

Step 2

Exporting your clients

On the “Contacts” page, select your clients and select the “Export” button to export all contacts to a CSV file.

Step 3

Import your clients

Once the file is downloaded, upload it to your Kivvik Wealth account via the Invite Client Page in the Signup Process, or by clicking Add A Client from the Dashboard.

IMPORT CLIENTS FROM REDTAIL

Boost the capabilities of Redtail’s dynamic system by pairing it with Kivvik’s robust “Know Your Client” system.

Step 1

Select your contacts

In the “Contacts” section, select the All Clients square checkbox to select all clients..

Step 2

Exporting Options

Click on the "Contact Options” and select “Exports” section to bring up the “Exports” modal.

Step 3

Export your clients

On the "Exports” modal, select “Default Settings” on the modal which triggers the download of the exported CSV file.

Step 4

Import your clients

Once the file is downloaded, upload it to your Kivvik Wealth account via the Invite Client Page in the Signup Process, or by clicking Add A Client from the Dashboard.

IMPORT CLIENTS FROM SALESFORCE

Boost the capabilities of Salesforce’s dynamic system by pairing it with Kivvik’s robust “Know Your Client” system.

Step 1

Reports

On your Salesforce Dashboard, click on “Reports” on the horizontal navigation bar.

Step 2

Select your report

On the Reports page, click on the “New Report…” button bringing up the Reports Modal.

Step 3

Make a new report

On the Reports Modal, click on the “Accounts & Contacts” folder to reveal the additional selections. Once the menu drops down, click on “Contacts & Accounts.” After this selection, click the “Create” button on the bottom right hand corner.

Step 4

Run the report

On the Unsaved Report page, click “Run Report” to export your contacts.

Step 5

Readying your report

Next, on the Contact Mailing List Report page, select “Export Details.”

Step 6

Exporting your clients

Lastly, on the Contact Mail List Report page, use the second drop down menu to select “Comma Delimited .csv” and select “Export” on the bottom right hand corner.

Step 7

Import your clients

Once the fitle is downloaded, upload it to your Kivvik Wealth account via the Invite Client Page in the Signup Process, or by clicking Add A Client from the Dashboard.
